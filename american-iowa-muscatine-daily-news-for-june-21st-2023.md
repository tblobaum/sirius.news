The Muscatine Journal website features a variety of news headlines. One article reports that math and reading scores among 13-year-olds in the United States have dropped to their lowest levels in decades, with math scores experiencing the largest decline ever recorded. Another article discusses a fire that destroyed a trailer, with the owner attributing her escape to her cats alerting her to the danger. The website also highlights the opening of the Gas Dock for the summer season, providing fuel and other services for boaters. Additionally, the Louisa County Board of Health listened to presentations on two preventative health programs, and Jefferson Elementary School began its SPARK summer program for students. The website also features a collection of historical photographs from the Mary E. Sawyer Auditorium in La Crosse and discusses the need for volunteers for the upcoming RAGBRAI event. Lastly, a burn ban has been issued in Muscatine County, prohibiting open burning until further notice, and funeral funding was approved during a recent meeting.
